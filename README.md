# margame 🛤️

*A natural language understood as a trained-disciplined-collective-path — the trodden way received as cosmic-gift before any walker adds a step: the Sanskrit-angle on the path.*

margame is two true roots welded into one word: Sanskrit **mārga** (मार्ग), "the trodden way" — the path made visible by repeated walking, with named stages (*aṅgas*), transmitted practice, and guru-disciple lineage — and Sumerian **me** (𒈨), the eldest word for a fundamental that was *received*, not made. So margame is the trodden-disciplined-way held as an ordinance: the path that has limbs and stages, undertaken as discipline and inherited from countless predecessors who left the trace. It is the cathedral's first explicit substantive-donor diversification under STOICHEIA — the forge that turned the donor from Greek toward Sanskrit.

margame is the co-sibling of **hodosme**: same realm-region, different donor-angle. Where hodosme foregrounds the *going* (the open journey, the way as road), margame foregrounds the *discipline* (the trained stages, the structured formation — the Eightfold Path, the three mārgas of the Gītā, Patañjali's eight-limbed yoga). Its light is the deep dignity of formation, the trace honored, the path received as gift; its shadow is rigid structure that crowns its own stages as the only road and coerces the walker who never freely chose to go. Forged in the cathedral at weighted **7.80/10**.
